Source Protection

At Limuru Water and Sewerage, our water is sourced from protected catchment areas. We employ 24/7 monitoring to ensure no contaminants enter the raw water supply.

Advanced Filtration

Using a multi-stage filtration process including sedimentation and sand filtration, we ensure Limuru's water exceeds international WHO standards for potable water.

Real-time Monitoring

Our SCADA system tracks pressure across the Limuru network, allowing us to detect leaks and pressure drops instantly to minimize service interruptions.

Quality Assurance

Every hour, our lab technicians test pH levels and chlorine residuals to guarantee that the water at your tap is safe for consumption.

Infrastructure Care

Limuru Water and Sewerage invests 30% of revenue into pipe replacement and reservoir cleaning to prevent rust and buildup within the municipal grid.

Community Outreach

Through our 'Water for Schools' initiative, we provide free storage tanks and hygiene education to over 50 local institutions every year.

The Limuru Water and Sewerage Operation Cycle

1. Intake & Screening: The process begins at our local reservoirs where large debris is screened out to protect our plant's mechanical components.

2. Coagulation & Flocculation: We add gentle chemicals that cause dirt particles to stick together (floc), making them heavy enough to sink.

3. Sedimentation: In large basins, the heavy "floc" particles settle, leaving clear water at the top ready for the filtration stage.

4. Disinfection: Before leaving the plant, a controlled amount of chlorine is added. This acts as a protective "shield" as the water travels through Limuru's underground pipes.

5. Distribution Management: We utilize gravity-fed systems combined with high-efficiency pumps to move water to high-altitude neighborhoods in Limuru.

6. Waste Management: As part of our sewerage mandate, sludge collected during treatment is processed into eco-friendly fertilizer for a zero-waste model.

7. Customer Feedback: Our AI-driven system prioritizes "No Water" reports across the Limuru service area to ensure rapid response times.
